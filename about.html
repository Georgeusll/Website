SDSU-G

San Diego State University – Georgia (SDSU-Georgia) and Tbilisi State University (TSU) held a joint event announcing admission of students into internationally accredited bachelor degree programs developed in partnership with San Diego State University, starting from 2020-2021 academic year. During the event the speakers have shared updates and news on granting international accreditation to these programs at partner universities, n particular the official ABET* accreditation of the TSU 2020 degree programs in Electrical and Electronics Engineering and Computer Science, as well as ACS** certification of biochemistry/chemistry program at TSU.
